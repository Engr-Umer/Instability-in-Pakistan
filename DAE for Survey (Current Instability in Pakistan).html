Survey on the Current Crisis in Pakistan: A Report by Intelli-Gen

Logo Image

Introduction

Intelli-Gen, a leading private research company, has conducted a comprehensive survey aimed at understanding the perspectives of Pakistani citizens regarding the current crisis in the country. The survey seeks to explore the 1,48,000 respondents' views on the government's performance, political responsibility, personal concerns, and potential resolutions to the crisis. By analyzing the data collected from this survey, we aim to identify patterns and trends that can help various stakeholders, including the government, opposition parties, civil society, and international organizations, to make informed decisions and take effective measures to address the ongoing challenges.


Current Instability in Pakistan

Pakistan is currently facing a crisis marked by economic instability, political turmoil, and social unrest. The survey focuses on capturing the sentiment of the population by asking questions related to the main reasons behind the crisis, the perceived responsibility of political parties and the establishment, and the effectiveness of the government's efforts to resolve the situation.


Uncertainty Among the People

The survey covers various aspects of uncertainty faced by the citizens, including concerns related to personal finances, safety, and Pakistan's

international reputation. Furthermore, the survey seeks to gauge the respondents' confidence in the future of Pakistan and their opinions on the appropriate timing for elections.


Purpose of the Survey

The primary objective of this survey is to provide an in-depth understanding of public sentiment during the current crisis in Pakistan. The insights gathered from the survey will help stakeholders in formulating strategies and making informed decisions to address the challenges faced by the country. The analysis will also enable civil society and the general public to engage in constructive discussions and contribute to finding solutions for the ongoing crisis.


Data Envelopment Analysis (DEA)

The data collected from this survey was analyzed using bivariate analysis techniques, which allowed us to explore the relationships between various categorical variables. We used Python programming language and popular data analysis libraries such as pandas, seaborn, and matplotlib to visualize these relationships. Some of the analysis conducted includes:

Univariate Analysis (Bar Graph & Pie Chart)

Bi-variate Analysis (Bar Graph & Stack Bar Plot)

These analyses provided valuable insights into the relationships between different factors, helping us identify patterns and trends that are essential for understanding the current crisis in Pakistan and guiding stakeholders towards potential solutions.


Conclusion

This report presents a summary of the findings from the survey conducted by Intelli-Gen on the current crisis in Pakistan. The data analyzed highlights the concerns and uncertainties faced by the citizens, as well as their perspectives on the government's performance, political responsibility, and the role of various stakeholders in addressing the crisis. We believe that this report will serve as a valuable resource for decision-makers, civil society, and the general public in their efforts to navigate and resolve the challenges faced by Pakistan during these turbulent times
